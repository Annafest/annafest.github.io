About.

Anna University.

Being one of India's oldest, yet foremost Engineering Colleges, Anna university is reputed for its plethora of knowledge and culture, for it encourages it's students to realise and accomplish their fullest potential. The University was formed by integrating four very well knows technical institutions : CEG, MIT, ACT and SAP; of the Chennai city. Since its formation, Anna University has not only pioneered in producing world class technocrats but also groomed valuable sportsmen for the country. In order to encourage sports, the university has exclusive courts for Hockey, Tennis, Basketball, Football, Volleyball, etc. Sportspersons are applauded, appreciated and awarded during the University Sports Day.

AUSB.

The Anna University Sports Board ensures the smooth and efficient conduction of various tournaments involving all the institutions under Anna University. AUSB consists of the Chairman, Faculty members and eminent sports personalities. The sole aim of AUSB is to recognise young talented sportsmen and give them an opportunity to represent the university at the National and International levels and bring laurels to the University, State and Nation. Anna University Sports Board constantly strives to achieve all round development of all its students so that they can serve the community and Nation!

Annafest.

Anna Sports Fest was first conducted in the year 2000, with a motive to encourage budding talents in various domains of sports. Apart from the above stated reasons, one of the most important aim of Anna Sports Fest is to harness core personalities such as tenacity, tolerance, compassion and to develop a cordial relationship among the players from various institutions. We believe that the Anna Sports Fest will serve as an ultimate arena for young warriors to groom and hone their skills in various sports by competing with students from other colleges.



Details and Rules.

Venue.

All the events will be conducted in the Guindy campus(CEG), Anna University except Badminton and Table Tennis which will be conducted in the MIT campus, Chromepet.

Time.

Entries will be closed and fixtures will be drawn for all games three days before the tournament date. Fixtures for all games will be drawn by lot basis only. The reporting time for the tournament is at 8:00 AM sharp on 12-2-2015.

Fixture.

Matches will be conducted as per fixtures framed. Teams participating in the game should report 30 minutes before the commencement. The presence of Team Managers is mandatory before, during and after the competition.

Food and Accommodation.

Accommodation will be provided only for the participating men teams, at the rate of Rs.100 per person. Food and Beverages will be available in the college canteen on payment.

Statutory Warning.

All the participating teams should follow the rules of CEG campus. Consumption and/or possession of Alcohol and Smoking is strictly prohibited. Team Managers are requested to ensure strict discipline among the participants.

Identity Proof.

Participants with valid ID cards will only be permitted inside the campuses. Spectators or outsiders or non-players cannot accompany the team participating in the tournament.

Eligibility.

The Eligibility of players shall be as per the AIU – AUSB rules in force. Details of players in the Entry Form should be signed by both Principal and Director of Physical Education of College concerned, and shall be produced to the organising committee on the day of the tournament.

Identity Card.

Individual ID cards of players duly signed by the Principal of the college concerned should be available throughout the days of the events, and shall be produced before the commencement of the game or on demand for verification and authentication purposes.

Rules.

The matches in each game shall be conducted according to the laws / rules in force of the concerned National Sports Federation. The decision of the referee in all the games is final. No protest shall be entertained on any account. The maximum entry for each game will be only 16 teams, except Cricket, for which it is only 8 teams.

Organising Committee.

The organising committee reserves the rights to change, alter or modify its venue or duration of the game for valid reasons.

Uniformity.

The team should compete in their distinct/ registered colours or uniform. Equipment will not be provided by the organisers of the tournament.

Sport Equipments.

Teams should bring balls/ shuttle cocks or such other such equipment which are approved by the respective National Sports Federation. “COSCO - Tournament” ball for Basketball / STALL 5 STAR for Table Tennis / YONEX shuttle cocks, etc.,
